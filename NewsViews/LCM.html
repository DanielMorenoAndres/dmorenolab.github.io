LiveCellMiner: A new tool to analyze mitotic progression

  • The recording of live cells by time-lapse microscopy has become a powerful tool to accurately identify the nature of mitotic and cell cycle defects in different experimental and pathological conditions. The quantitative and unbiased analysis of cellular phenotypes in these recordings is a challenge that has been addressed in recent years. This has happened thanks to advances in computing power, but above all to the emergence of several methodologies and software tools that have automated the analysis process, covering and improving the different needs to answer biological questions.
  • In this paper, using cell mitosis as a test bench, we present LiveCellMiner, a new tool for time-dependent quantitative analysis of the cell cycle.
