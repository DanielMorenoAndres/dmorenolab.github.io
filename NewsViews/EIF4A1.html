The DEAD-box helicase eIF4A1/2 acts as RNA chaperone during mitotic exit enabling chromatin decondensation

  • Our new work on how DEAD-box helicase eIF4A1/2 regulates the composition and fluidity of the mitotic perichromatin layer has just been published.
  • This moonlighting activity of eIF4A1/2 is independent of its primary function as a transcription factor and is crucial for the dynamic reorganization of chromatin as cells exit mitosis.
