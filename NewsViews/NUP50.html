The nucleoporin Nup50 activates the Ran guanine nucleotide exchange factor RCC1 to promote NPC assembly at the end of mitosis

  • Find now the last contribution from our institute at the EMBO Journal. In this work, leaded by G. Holzer, we define a new role of the nucleoporin Nup50 in the assembly of the nuclear pore complex, which adds up to its already known function in nuclear transport.
