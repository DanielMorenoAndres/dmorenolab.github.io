open positions

We have no suitable PhD or Postdoc positions at the moment. However, if you would like to join our lab, contact us submitting your application. It should include your CV, a description of your personal motivation and research interests, as well as a short summary of your academic goals. We are always looking for candidates interested in our research, who bring or aim to apply for funding through external fellowships (find some individual funding opportunities here, here or here).

We always look for students (biology, biotechnology, biochemistry, chemistry, biomedical engineering, and other related disciplines) willing to do their bachelor or master thesis in our lab studying mitosis in Hematopoietic disorders or nuclear reformation at the end of mitosis. Our approaches use state-of-the-art tissue culture, immunofluorescence, confocal microscopy, life-cell imaging, as well as classical biochemical methodologies. If you are interested, just contact us sending a short motivation letter, a current overview of your grades and your CV.
